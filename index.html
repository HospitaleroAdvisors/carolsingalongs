What is a carol sing-along? +
Most of them follow a simple, welcoming format - you gather, sing carols together for 45 minutes to an hour, then coffee and cookies and conversation. Some events have a special twist - like the Lessons and Carols service where singing carols together is woven in between Bible readings telling the Christmas story.
What do you get out of a sing-along? +
You'll experience three big things. First - they're FUN! There's something joyful about being in a room full of people belting out 'Jingle Bells' and 'Deck the Halls' together. Then there's the warm hospitality of the host communities and being IN the music rather than just watching a performance.
